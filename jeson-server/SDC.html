Service Delivery Center (SDC)

Help & Instructions

Welcome to the Service Delivery Center! Please follow these steps:

  1. Select one of the counter buttons on the left to initiate a service request.
  2. Each button corresponds to a different service. Choose the one that fits your needs.
  3. After selecting a counter, follow any on-screen instructions to complete your request.
  4. If you need to log out, click the "Log Out" button in the header.
  5. For further assistance, please contact customer support.
